I am honored to share that I was recognized as one of EqualityX's Top 50 Influential Muslims of 2024 in the Americas. This recognition is awarded to Muslim professionals who have authentically embraced their faith in the workplace, and through their efforts have brought about positive impact in their communities.

In my work at West Monroe I sought out community through the development and founding of our Interfaith ERG. It has become a phenomenal platform to increase awareness and inclusiveness of Muslim colleagues alongside those of other faiths. I was one of our cofounders of the ERG, which launched in 2021.
Founder of Interfaith ERG@West Monroe

I am proud to have founded the Interfaith Employee Resource Group (ERG) at West Monroe. This initiative has provided a platform to foster inclusivity and awareness of diverse faiths in the workplace. Through this ERG, we have created a supportive community that celebrates and respects the beliefs of all employees, promoting a culture of understanding and collaboration.

It is a privilege to have the opportunity to build community within every space I am in - from the workplace to the masjid to my home.
